NBA Basketball Adventure - Overview

An official NBA basketball-themed simulator Roblox game. Shoot basketballs to defeat opponents and collect chips to unlock new powerups and upgrade your basketballs as you platform through each level. This game was developed by Floor 84 Studio in collaboration with NBA.

Team Assets Swapping

Assets and UGC are swapped according to user preferences. All team-specific aspects of the map are changed without delay.

Drip Rewards

Shards are gradually dropped as the player depletes the opponent's health. This keeps the player engaged and committed to finishing the opponent.

Portals

Portals are scattered throughout the map for quick access to the hub. The hub also has a portal that sends the player to their previous zone.

Locked Vending Machines

Different tiered gacha vending machines locked based on progression. Prevents players from purchasing stronger basketballs meant for later stages of the game.
